Top Artists Word Analysis

When I went and evaluated this set from the top artists, I wasn't much surprised. The results are pretty much exactly the same as the all artists, with an exception. The words "dont" and "know" switched places it seems, leading me to think that the top artists have an even lesser idea about love than the others. Their gain to popularity might be because the major part of the population might not know what love is either (cue the song "I wanna know what love is" here). Having said that though, I can't say I'm surprised because the Top Artists would probably have the most dominant say on what is popular in terms of musical topics.
